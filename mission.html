How is Waste turned into Watts?

PowerSprout is an IoT-powered, single-chamber Microbial Fuel Cell (MFC) designed for large-scale deployment. By combining electrogenic bacteria and a network of smart sensors, it turns food waste into clean, storable energy.

  4. IoT sensors monitor voltage, gas release, temperature, pH, microbial health & system load in real time.
