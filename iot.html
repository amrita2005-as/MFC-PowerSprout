pH Sensor
Monitors acidity to maintain ideal conditions for microbes.

LCD Displays pH and Temperature values.
Temperature Sensor Monitors the temperature of the system for bacterial efficiency.
Air Cathode Receives electrons to complete the power circuit.
Anode Microbes release electrons during breakdown.

Super Capacitor Stores Electricity Generated.
