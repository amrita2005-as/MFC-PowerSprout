About PowerSprout

PowerSprout is an innovative, IoT-enabled Microbial Fuel Cell (MFC) system that converts food waste into clean electricity. Designed as a sustainable waste-to-energy solution, PowerSprout uses electrogenic bacteria to break down organic matter in an anaerobic chamber, releasing electrons captured to generate power. With real-time monitoring via sensors and energy stored in a supercapacitor, it offers smart, low-carbon energy generation ideal for eco-conscious communities and smart city initiatives.
